# FAQ Section

### "My QuickBooks is a total disaster. Is it too far gone to fix?"
Honestly? Probably not. We’ve seen it all—years of missing data, mixed personal/business expenses, negative bank balances. We specialize in untangling the knots. It’s fixable, and we can fix it.

### "What if I’m embarrassed to show you my books?"
Please don’t be! We are a judgment-free zone. Doctors don't judge you for being sick, and we don't judge you for having messy books. We are here to help, not to grade you.

### "Do you work with my accountant at tax time?"
Yes! We don’t replace your CPA or tax filer; we make their life (and yours) infinitely easier. We provide the clean, organized financial reports they need to file your taxes accurately and on time.

### "Do I have to already be using QuickBooks?"
Not at all. If you’re currently using a spreadsheet (or a shoebox), we can set you up with QuickBooks Online from scratch and import your data correctly from day one.

### "How do we work together if I’m not in Scranton?"
We work 100% remotely using secure cloud technology. We can access your QuickBooks Online securely, and we communicate via email and Zoom. We serve clients in PA and beyond without ever needing to step into your office.
