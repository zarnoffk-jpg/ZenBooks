# About Zen Books

**We’re Here to Take the Stress Out of Your Numbers.**

Zen Books is a husband-and-wife bookkeeping team based in Scranton, Pennsylvania, dedicated to bringing calm, judgment-free financial clarity to small business owners across the U.S. We specialize in helping **Real Estate Investors** and **Therapists & Consultants** stop worrying about their books and start trusting their numbers.

**The Team Behind Your Books**

We believe that great bookkeeping requires both technical precision and human understanding.

*   **She is the QuickBooks Expert:** The technical wizard who loves turning chaotic data into perfect rows and columns, handling the cleanup, categorization, and complex reconciliations.
*   **He is the Client Partner:** The operations lead who ensures every question is answered in plain English, every deadline is met, and you always feel supported, not just "processed."

Together, we provide a seamless, supportive experience that feels less like a chore and more like a partnership.

**Why We Started**

We saw too many talented business owners treating their bookkeeping like a shameful secret—hiding from their numbers, dreading tax time, and feeling embarrassed to ask for help. We built Zen Books to change that. We wanted to create a safe place where you can hand over a messy file without fear of judgment and get back a clear, organized system.

**Our Promise to You**

*   **Zero Judgment, Ever.** We don’t care how messy your books are right now. We only care about getting them clean for your future.
*   **Plain English, No Jargon.** We explain your reports simply so you actually understand what your business is doing.
*   **Reliable Consistency.** You’ll get accurate reports on time, every time, so you’re never scrambling before a deadline.

**Your Books Are Safe With Us.**

Whether you’re years behind or just starting out, we’re ready to help. Let’s turn that stress into clarity.
