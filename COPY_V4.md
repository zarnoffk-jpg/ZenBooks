# B) Updated Package Naming & Positioning

**1. One-Time Bookkeeping Cleanup & Catch-Up**
*   **Who it's for:** Business owners who are months (or years) behind and dread opening their finance apps.
*   **Benefits:**
    *   We categorize every past transaction so your history is accurate.
    *   We reconcile all bank and credit card accounts to the penny.
    *   We fix errors and "mystery" balances so you start fresh.
*   **The "Hand-off" Promise:** You hand us the mess once; we turn it into a clean, organized system you never have to worry about again.

**2. Monthly Bookkeeping Care**
*   **Who it's for:** Busy owners who want their books done correctly every single month without lifting a finger.
*   **Benefits:**
    *   Monthly categorization of all income and expenses.
    *   Reconciliation of all accounts to ensure accuracy.
    *   Simple, clear monthly reports delivered to your inbox.
*   **The "Hand-off" Promise:** We handle the monthly chore quietly in the background. You just read the report and get back to work.

**3. Premium Bookkeeping Support**
*   **Who it's for:** Growing businesses with complex needs like payroll, multiple entities, or grant tracking.
*   **Benefits:**
    *   Detailed reporting (KPIs, budget vs. actuals).
    *   Regular check-in calls to review performance.
    *   Coordination with your CPA, payroll provider, or board.
*   **The "Hand-off" Promise:** Think of us as your partner keeping an eye on the numbers so you can focus entirely on growth.

*(Note: We use QuickBooks Online exclusively for all packages because it’s the industry standard your CPA loves—but you don’t need to be the expert. We are.)*

# C) "Who We Work With" Section Copy

**Real Estate Investors**
You juggle multiple properties, loans, and entities. We keep your books clean by property so you always know your true cash flow and are ready for lenders or tax time. You focus on the deals; we’ll track the dollars.

**Therapists & Consultants**
You want to stay in session, not in spreadsheets. We organize your income and expenses so you know exactly what you’re taking home. Hand off the admin work and focus on your clients.

**Nonprofits**
You answer to a board and manage restricted funds or grants. We provide the clean, transparent reporting you need for compliance and confidence, so you can focus on your mission, not the math.

**Restaurants & Construction**
Your operations are intense—vendors, payroll, daily transactions. You don’t have time to sit at a computer reconciling receipts. We handle the high volume of transactions so you can stay on the job site or in the kitchen.
